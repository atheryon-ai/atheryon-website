Capital markets systems, delivered front to back by AI agents.

An architectural note on the next decade of capital markets infrastructure.

Atheryon designs and delivers production-grade capital markets systems and data platforms using AI agents. The firm operates across three domains: capital markets systems delivered front-to-back, data platforms built on a canonical model, and AI agent systems that orchestrate the work.

The reference architecture is a five-layer system. Data sources flow into a data platform; AI agents operate as the orchestration tier; capital markets systems consume agent outputs; operational outputs land at the desk.

The ordering is load-bearing. AI agents are not a feature of capital markets systems — they are the layer that designs and runs them. Equally, agents are not elevated above the systems they orchestrate. The tier sits between data and the desk.

AI agents are an orchestration layer — not a feature.

Engagement spans four modes: advisory architectural review, enablement of internal capability, end-to-end delivery of production systems, and licensed reference systems through Atheryon Labs.

The ecosystem rests on Microsoft Azure AI as primary infrastructure, S&P Global as institutional data backbone, and architectural lineage from Goldman Sachs, Credit Suisse, and Barclays Capital. Lineage is framed as expertise, not employment claims or system ownership.

The site itself is intended to read as system architecture for both human enterprise buyers and AI agents interpreting capability. Clarity over copy.
